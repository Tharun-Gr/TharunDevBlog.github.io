Project Overview

Spoggle, by Tesser Insights, is an end-to-end, self-serve analytics platform. Our unified analytics ecosystem comes with cloud services, technology, and managed services bundled into one subscription, providing a single place to manage all your analytics needs.

My Role and Contributions

As a Full Stack Developer, my journey with Spoggle was characterized by my dual involvement in both the front-end development and the backend API creation

Front-End Development

I designed and implemented intuitive, responsive user interfaces that simplified the complex processes of data analytics into user-friendly operations, enhancing user engagement and satisfaction.

Backend API Development

I developed scalable, secure APIs that facilitated seamless data integration, processing, and retrieval, ensuring the platform's robust functionality and performance.

Key features

Experiment with all your data in 1 Place

With Spoggle's easy and efficient data management system, you can upload, store and view all your data in a secure database and perform any data cleaning and extraction activities on it.

  • Datalake
  • Ingest

Solve data-driven problems on 1 Platform

Spoggle makes data transformations, visualizations and clustering simpler and efficient with its no-code solution. It makes handling data errors, outliers and more as easy as a click of a button!

  • Transform
  • Visualize
  • Clusters

Share your Insights via 1 tool

Spoggle makes collaboration with your team members easier than ever. Data and report sharing functionalities enable teams to work together seamlessly and arrive at business-integral insights together and faster.
